Bibliothekai

In Ancient Greek, βιβλιοθῆκαι means bookcases, or more specifically a library.

Here, we collect information on ancient texts - primarily, but not exclusively, Greek and Latin works - and the translations available into modern languages. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive resource for finding, evaluating, and selecting translations of classical works.

Please be aware that this site is under heavy development and considered alpha stage. Please contact David Reed with any questions.
